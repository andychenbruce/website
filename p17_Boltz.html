The equation for the maxwell boltzman distribution is

$$ f(v) = {({m \over 2\pi kT})^{3/2}}4\pi v^2e^{-{{mv^2} \over {2kT}}}$$
where
$f(v)$ is the probability
$v$ is the velocity
$m$ is the mass
$k$ is the boltzmann constant
$T$ is the temperature
